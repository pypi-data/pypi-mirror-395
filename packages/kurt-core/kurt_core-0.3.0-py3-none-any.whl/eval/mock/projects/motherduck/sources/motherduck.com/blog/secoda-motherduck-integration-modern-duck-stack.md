## Setting up the integration

[Integrating MotherDuck with Secoda](https://docs.secoda.co/integrations/data-warehouses/motherduck) takes two easy steps -

**1\. Retrieve your MotherDuck service token**

- Navigate to your MotherDuck instance you want to connect
- Click on your profile in the top right corner
- Click on settings from the dropdown
- Under the General tab, click the 'Copy token' button

**2\. Connect MotherDuck to Secoda**

- In the Secoda App, select â€˜Add Integrationâ€™ on the Integrations tab
- Search for and select MotherDuck
- Enter your service token that you have copied
- Click connect
