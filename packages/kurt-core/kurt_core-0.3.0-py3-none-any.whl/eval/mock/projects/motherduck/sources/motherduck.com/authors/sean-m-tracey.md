# Sean M. Tracey

Head of Developer Relations at Expanso

Sean M. Tracey is the Head of Developer Relations at Expanso, the platform that provides global compute for your data, wherever you want it: cross-cloud, cross-region, at the edge, or on-premises. It allows you to have all the flexibility of a global cloud platform, while still keeping your data on your premises.

A seasoned technologist, Sean is a tenured writer and the author of several programming books. Previously, he has worked at AWS, IBM, and FT Labs as a software engineer, creative technologist, and developer advocate.
