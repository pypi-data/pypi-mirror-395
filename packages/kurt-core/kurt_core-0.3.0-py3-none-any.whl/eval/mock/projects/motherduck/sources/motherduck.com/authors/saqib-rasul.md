# Saqib Rasul

Saqib Rasul is the CTO of Geobase, where he currently leads its development. He started his career as an early engineer at 360T, helping it achieve a €500 million acquisition by Deutsche Börse. Saquib holds dual major degrees in Aerospace and Software Engineering from RMIT. By night, he is a standup comic based out of Frankfurt, Germany.
